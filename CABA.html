Welcome to to CABA(California Basketball Association)!

The CABA is the best basketball association for all ages that is run by kids. There is a small fee of $3.00 for children under 3 years old & a fee of $5.00 for children over 3 years old. Adults have a fee of $10.00. Spectaters can watch for free, but can not interfere in games(eg : Holding the ball).

Raffle : *The monthly raffle winners get to go to CABA without any fees. They will get a pass to do so. You can get a ticket when you donate more than $10.00 to CABA. Every $10.00 is worth a ticket. During Black Friday sales, a ticket is worth $5.00, & if you buy 10 tickets($50), you get 3 free tickets.
